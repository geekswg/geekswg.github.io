{{< admonition tip "添加您的 FixIt 网站" >}}
您可以通过 [创建 PR :(fa-solid fa-code-branch fa-fw):](https://github.com/geekswg/blog-fixit/pulls) 或 [编辑数据 :(fa-regular fa-pen-to-square fa-fw):](https://github.com/geekswg/blog-fixit/edit/master/data/friends.yml)  按 **nickname** 以字典顺序将您的 FixIt 网站添加到此页面，格式如下：

```yml

  avatar: <your avatar>
  url: <your site link>
  description: <description of your site>
```
